How to Play

You find yourself lost in the darkness. You feel faint, you're falling, you're blacking out. You need a burst, and better soon. There's nothing like music to make you feel alive again.

Your energy gauge looks like this:
Fill it up by collecting the glow orbs to power up, and you can shoot enemies. But make sure you don't stray from your path. If you do, you'll lose points and earn a lower score.

Grab to stack up more points.

Controls

  • MOUSE to move around the screen
  • LEFT-CLICK to shoot enemies within your attack range
  • ESC KEY to quit the game at any time
  • Refresh the page (F5 KEY) to start over after you reach the High Scores

Supported Audio Files

MP3, FLAC, WAV
